Proteja seus Painéis Solares contra Pombos e Pragas  

Os pombos estão causando danos nos seus painéis solares e no seu telhado? Não se preocupe, podemos ajudar! Nós removemos os pombos de forma humanitária, limpamos a sujeira nos seus painéis solares, telhado e nas áreas ao redor, e em seguida instalamos uma tela de arame galvanizado revestido em PVC, de 6" ou 8", ao longo de todo o perímetro dos seus painéis solares.

Nossa tela de arame de aço de 18 gauge é projetada para impedir que aves ou outros animais acessem a área abaixo dos painéis solares. Com a instalação personalizada da tela e atenção aos detalhes para evitar que eles façam ninhos, aves de todos os tamanhos não conseguem se esconder ou nidificar debaixo dos seus painéis solares.

  • The cleaner your solar panels are, the more energy they produce. It is generally agreed upon that dirty and soiled panels can lose up to 20% of renewable energy production. Most solar panels require at least annual cleaning, though cleaning them more often is not uncommon. Remember that solar panels are electrically charged and naturally attract dust, therefore the general rule of solar panel maintenance is to have them cleaned and inspected every six months. Solar panel cleaning frequency also depends on other factors such as the natural environment. Are they located in an area that's windy and dusty? Are they located next to a freeway or airport? Are there pigeons on the roof or under the solar panels making a mess on them? And even the slope of the roof can have a dramatic affect on cleanliness and production. Are they installed on a high-pitched roof? Or are they installed on a flatter low-pitched roof? The flatter the roof, the faster they get dirty.

  • There is a well established misconception that solar panel cleaning is unnecessary because the rain will clean them. This is false. Have you ever owned a car, especially a dark colored one? You know first hand how quickly these dark painted surfaces accumulate dirt and grime very quickly. The dark glass of the solar panels are similar. Just in the way that rain never cleaned a dark colored car, the rain will also not clean solar panels. Why? This is because just like a car, the glass of the solar panel needs to be agitated and routinely scrubbed off before these surfaces will get clean once again.
